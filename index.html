Sadaham Uyana

Sinhala Buddhist Devotional App

About the App

Sadaham Uyana is a Sinhala Buddhist devotional app that includes Budu Gatha, Pirith, Seth Kawi and Buddhist prayers in one place. The app also provides Pirith and Suthra audio for daily listening.
